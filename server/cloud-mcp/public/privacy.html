Forge3D Cloud — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026 · Contact: giovan.ruiz.000@gmail.com

Forge3D Cloud is a Model Context Protocol (MCP) connector that lets an AI assistant design 3D-printable electromechanical products. This policy explains what it does with your data.

What we process

  • Design requests — the goals and tool arguments you send (e.g. "a sumo robot with 4 motors"). These are processed in memory to generate a design and are not stored after the response is returned.
  • Identity — when you sign in via OAuth, we receive your identity provider's subject identifier. We use it only to route requests to your paired desktop and never to build a profile.
  • Operational logs — standard server logs (timestamps, request paths, errors) for reliability and abuse prevention. These do not contain your design content.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not persist your designs, prompts, or generated files server-side.
  • We do not train models on your content.

Third parties

Authentication is handled by your configured OAuth identity provider, subject to its own privacy policy. The connector runs on infrastructure operated by the project owner.

Data retention

Design content is transient (held only for the duration of a request). Logs are retained briefly for operational purposes and then rotated.

Your choices

You can stop using the connector at any time by removing it in your AI client and revoking access in your identity provider. For questions or deletion requests, contact the email above.
