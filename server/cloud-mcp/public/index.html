② Drive your installed app live

So Claude builds right inside your Forge3D 3D viewport.

  1. Open the Forge3D app (download above) and sign in / continue offline.
  2. On the home screen, click “Connect to Claude” (top-right) → Turn on the bridge.
  3. Click Copy config — it copies a small MCP snippet.
  4. Paste it into your Claude MCP config (Claude Desktop: Settings → Developer → Edit config; Claude Code: your .mcp.json), then restart Claude.
  5. Ask Claude to build — it appears live in your open app.
