About Forge3D

Turn a sentence into a real, 3D-printable gadget.

Forge3D is an AI maker studio that closes the whole loop from idea to hardware. Describe what you want — "a sumo robot with ultrasonic, 4 motors and an Arduino" — and Forge3D designs it: the 3D geometry, an auto-wired circuit, the firmware, a bill of materials, and it even runs printability and physics checks so what you build actually works.

What makes it different

  • Design + electronics together — model parts and lay out circuits side by side, at real millimetre scale.
  • Orchestra AI director — one sentence in, a whole validated build out; it plans, builds, wires and tests.
  • Drive it from Claude — connect Forge3D to Claude and let it build live in your 3D viewport.
  • Simulate before you build — power the circuit, run the firmware, and watch real-world physics.
  • Export to fabricate — one click to STL, a sticker-circuit SVG, and a BOM.

Our mission

Make hardware as easy to create as software. Forge3D removes the gap between an idea and a working, printable, wired-up product — so anyone can go from a thought to a fabricated gadget in an afternoon.
